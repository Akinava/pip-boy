10 PB7  OSC2
11 PD5  LED

13 PD7  Button Right

15 PB1  Button Up
16 PB2  SD CS
17 PB3  MOSI
18 PB4  MISO
19 PB5  SCK
20 VCC  VCC
21 AREF VCC
22 GND  GND

25 PC2

=== Oled
GND GND
VCC VCC
SCL PC5

=== Buzzer

=== LED

=== SD Card
CS   - PB2
SCK  - SCK
MOSI - MOSI
MISO - resistor - MISO
VCC  - VCC
GND  - GND

=== Crystal
GND + capacitor ----+----- OSC1
    |            crystal
    + capacitor ----+----- OSC2
